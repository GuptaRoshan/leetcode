1. Permutations

Formula  : P(n,r) = (n−r)! / n!

Permutations of 2 letters from ABC:

P(3,2) = (3−2)! / 3! = 3×2×1 =6
The permutations are: AB, BA, AC, CA, BC, CB.

------------------------------------------------

2. Combination

Formula : C(n,r) = n! / r!×(n−r)!

Combinations of 2 letters from ABC:

C(3,2) = 3! / 2!×(3−2)! = 3×2×1 / 2×1×1 =3
The combinations are: AB, AC, BC.
